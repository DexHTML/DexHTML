Автор и инициатор цифровых проектов. Проектирую внутренние инструменты, сервисные системы, контентные платформы и рабочие прототипы с использованием web- и AI-инструментов.

## Key projects

### TechRepair

Private internal system for equipment tracking and repair workflows.

Focus areas:
- equipment cards;
- QR-based identification;
- repair history;
- directories;
- reports;
- action audit;
- integration-ready architecture.

### ServiceHub / FairDesk

Private internal request-management ecosystem.

Focus areas:
- requester and executor roles;
- ticket lifecycle;
- workload-aware assignment;
- equipment integration;
- schedule integration;
- assignment explanation;
- archive and audit.

### Duty Schedule

Private internal planning tool for duty shifts and employee availability.

Focus areas:
- employee groups;
- duty queue;
- vacations;
- production calendar;
- compensation days;
- manual overrides;
- Excel export;
- future integration with ticket assignment.
